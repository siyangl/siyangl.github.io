Biography

I am a software engineer at Google AI Perception. I received my Ph.D. in Electrial Engineering at the University of Southern California advised by Prof. C.-C. Jay Kuo . Before that, I received my B. Eng. degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Edmund Y. Lam.

My primary research interest lies in machine learning, deep learning for object localization. More information is available in my curriculum vitae.
